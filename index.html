— About the Game

Plain Anchor is a strategic survival game where players balance exploration and stability. Drifting allows discovery of new ocean sectors and resources, while anchoring provides safety and system recovery. It's all about knowing when to move and when to stay put - mess up your timing and you'll run out of everything.

— FAQ

Essential insights for navigating the deep. Consult the archives before the next tide rolls in.

  • Is Plain Anchor action-heavy?
    No. It focuses on strategy and survival pacing.
  • Is there combat?
    It's you versus the ocean - no enemies to fight, just nature trying to kill you.
  • Does difficulty increase?
    Yes. Weather patterns and resource scarcity intensify over time.
  • Is it suitable for short sessions?
    Yes. You can play in short bursts - each session feels complete.
